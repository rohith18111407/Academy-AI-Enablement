Title: Employee Reimbursement Policy

Employees can claim reimbursements for business-related expenses.

Eligible expenses include:
- Travel (flight, hotel, taxi)
- Meals during business travel
- Client entertainment (with approval)

Reimbursement process:
1. Submit expense report via Finance Portal.
2. Attach original receipts.
3. Manager approval required.
4. Finance team processes payment within 10 business days.
